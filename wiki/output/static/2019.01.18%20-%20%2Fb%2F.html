/b/ Self-Dialectic

Anonymity against whom? Relatedly, privacy against whom? It comes in many dok.

Do you anonymize parts of yourselves against other parts of yourselves? How will you tell yourselves the stories of yourselves, and what makes the story Good? This is Self-Dialectic. I don't see any simple answers. I suggest T42T must be played even at that fragmented, Frankfurtian personal identity level. This is what it is like to game-theoretically constitute yourself with your self-chosen principles to generate what Korsgaard roughly calls an integrated self. The various kinds of denial of one's 4DID is a hard problem. It's Parfit, Ship of Theseus, Buddhism, etc. Those structures define a great deal about The Existential Game.
