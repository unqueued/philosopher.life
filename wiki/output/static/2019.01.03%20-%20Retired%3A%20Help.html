Essentially, this wiki is an accountability-based, high-transparency, cryptographically verifiable implementation of The Categorical Imperative. Everything I say here can and will be used against me. So, while I respect privacy rights (and find them exceedingly necessary for our world), I'm electing to relinquish mine because I feel compelled by practical wisdom applied in my context.
