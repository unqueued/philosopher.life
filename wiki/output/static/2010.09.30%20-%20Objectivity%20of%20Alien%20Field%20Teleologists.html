What I considered most profound was how this thought experiment points out some very serious flaws in naturalistic accounts (more narrow than just a teleological account, as property dualism can theoretically do just this) of intentionality, and arguably, I think the thought experiment may detract from (rather than support) Okrent‚Äôs conclusions.
